CHILDREN More Precious than Gold
Few resources on Earth are as scarce—or as powerful—as a fully developed human intellect. That’s one of the reasons the Shakti Foundation chose this extraordinary resource as the foundation for its global currency. We also recognize that children—the most precious blessings of humanity—represent the purest form of this potential. It’s no coincidence that our name, “Shakti,” is drawn from ancient Sanskrit, referring to the innate life-force and creative power present in all people.  Unlike other cryptocurrencies, Shakti Coin is not mined through computational achievement, but rather through human achievement in the advancement of education. We see this as the most direct way to help each person, and each community, achieve their true potential. The Shakti Project is a grassroots initiative—started by dedicated volunteer parents, educators, and blockchain enthusiasts who believe that currency can be a catalyst for social change.

But making that change will require a new kind of global currency—one that is easy, instant, and inexpensive to use. Help us make a difference. Help us make history. If you share our vision of what currency could be, we invite you to join us.

S H A K T I vs F I A T
It's simply better money. Most fiat currencies work well for governments and financial institutions, a lot less well for the average person, and not at all for the 2 billion people who are "unbanked". Shakti Coin is changing that with a global currency that works for all of us.
Money without Middlemen Today's centralized financial institutions contribute friction and fees. There is a better way.
Faster Transactions Blockchain-based transactions with Shakti are instant and irreversible.
Hightened Security Currency within the blockchain is protected from theft, counterfeiting, or doctoring.
Remittance Relief Sending money can cost from 10- 20% and take forever. Shakti has instant transfers for less than the cost of a postage stamp.
Protecting your privacy Centralized systems pool your data, making it vulnerable to theft and abuse. A distributed ledger avoids this risk.
No Transaction Too Small The cost of using fiat currency makes small transactions unfeasible. Blockchain supports tiny transactions with ease.
No Exchange Rates Exchange rates make moving money over borders unpredictable and costly. Not so with a global blockchain-based currency.
Made for Micro-Loans Smart Shakti contracts on the blockchain make it just as easy to loan a dollar as it is a million.
